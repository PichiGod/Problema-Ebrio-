Drunkard's Problem

Minimum: 1 ~ Maximum: 9999

Problematic

Let's suppose a drunkard is standing on a street corner, deciding to walk around to let the "you know what" effect wear off.


Let's assume there is an equal probability that he will go north, south, east, or west at each street corner.


If he walks 10 streets, what is the probability that he will end up two streets away from where he started?
